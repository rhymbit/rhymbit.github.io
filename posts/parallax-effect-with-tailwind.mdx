## Parallax Effect

Before testing this out, remember to replace `image-url` with the URL of the image you want to use as the background.

<MdxCode>
```html
<div class="relative h-screen overflow-y-auto overflow-x-hidden perspective-[0.5px]">
  <!-- Parallax background -->
  <div 
    class="absolute inset-0 -z-10 translate-z-[-1px] scale-200 bg-center bg-cover" 
    style="background-image: url('image-url');">
  </div>

  <!-- Content -->
  <section class="min-h-screen flex items-center justify-center text-white text-7xl font-black">
    Parallax Effect
  </section>

  <section class="min-h-screen bg-black text-slate-200 flex items-center justify-center text-6xl">
    2nd Page as a Filler
  </section>
</div>
```
</MdxCode>
